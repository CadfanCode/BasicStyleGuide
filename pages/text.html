Text

Below are examples of standard paragraph text and how it should be used.

Example Paragraph

This is an example of a paragraph. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Donec euismod, nisl eget consectetur sagittis, nisl nunc.

<p>This is an example of a paragraph. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Donec euismod, nisl eget consectetur sagittis, nisl nunc.</p>

Multiple Paragraphs

Paragraphs should be separated by blank lines in HTML for readability:

First paragraph of text. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.

Second paragraph of text. Donec euismod, nisl eget consectetur sagittis, nisl nunc.


<p>First paragraph of text. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</p>
<p>Second paragraph of text. Donec euismod, nisl eget consectetur sagittis, nisl nunc.</p>

Text Formatting

You can style your text using emphasis, italics, or strong importance:


<p>This is <em>emphasized</em>, this is <i>italic</i>, and this is <strong>strong</strong> text.</p>

Line Breaks

Use the <br> tag to insert a line break within a paragraph:

First line of text.
Second line of text after a break.

<p>First line of text.<br>Second line of text after a break.</p>
