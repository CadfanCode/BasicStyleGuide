Paneler

Below are examples of different types of panels based on their content.

Positiv Panel

Success! Everything is OK.

Your action has been completed successfully. You can proceed with the next step.

<div class="panel positive">
    <h3>Success! Everything is OK.</h3>
    <p>Your action has been completed successfully. You can proceed with the next step.</p>
</div>

Neutral Panel

Info: Your current status.

This is an informational message. There is nothing urgent here, just some basic updates.

<div class="panel neutral">
    <h3>Info: Your current status.</h3>
    <p>This is an informational message. There is nothing urgent here, just some basic updates.</p>
</div>

Negativ Panel

Error: Something went wrong!

There was an issue with your request. Please try again later or contact support.

<div class="panel negative">
    <h3>Error: Something went wrong!</h3>
    <p>There was an issue with your request. Please try again later or contact support.</p>
</div>

Best practice:

  • Use a Positiv Panel for success messages and to confirm actions.
  • Use a Neutral Panel for general information or updates.
  • Use a Negativ Panel for errors or alerts that need attention.
